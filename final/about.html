About Artisan Aromas

Crafting moments of tranquility through the art of candle making

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Artisan Aromas began as a passion project in a small home studio. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a mission to create candles that not only illuminate spaces but also elevate the human experience through carefully crafted fragrances and sustainable practices.

Every candle we create tells a story of dedication, quality, and environmental consciousness. We believe that the simple act of lighting a candle can transform an ordinary moment into something extraordinary.

Our team of skilled artisans hand-pours each candle using premium soy wax and thoughtfully selected fragrance oils. From concept to completion, we maintain the highest standards of craftsmanship to ensure every candle meets our exacting quality standards.

Our Values

Quality Craftsmanship

We never compromise on quality. Each candle is meticulously crafted to ensure consistent burn quality and fragrance throw.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices, using renewable soy wax, cotton wicks, and recyclable packaging materials.

Innovation

We continuously explore new fragrance combinations and techniques to bring you unique and captivating scent experiences.

Community

We believe in supporting our local community and building lasting relationships with our customers and suppliers.
