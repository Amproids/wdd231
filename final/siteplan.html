Artisan Aromas Site Plan

Site Name

Artisan Aromas

This name was selected because it emphasizes the handcrafted nature of our products and highlights the aromatic experience our soaps and candles provide.

Site Purpose

Artisan Aromas is a store for selling good smelling products made with essential oils. The site provides customers with information about our handcrafted soaps and candles, ingredient details, and an easy way to browse and purchase our products.

Scenarios

  • "What ingredients are in your soaps and are they safe for sensitive skin?"
  • "How long do your candles burn and what scents do you offer?"
